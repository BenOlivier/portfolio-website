Photogrammetry

I am fascinated by photogrammetry (the process of constructing 3D models from photographs). I have developed a workflow to scan objects in high detail. Flashes and cross-polarised light allow me to take sharp photos which in turn generates accurate scans.

NFT

I have dabbled with creating my own 3D NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. I aim for each model's textures to be devoid of any baked-in lighting so that they can be placed in any potential lighting condition. In this sense they are 'metaverse ready' assets.

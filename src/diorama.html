Diorama

Diorama is our filmmaking app for the Litho controller. We asked ourselves, what if you had access to Hollywood-grade VFX in your pocket, and the whole world was your set?

Diorama allows you to construct a scene in augmented reality made of 3D objects and filters created by artists, and then record a video of your creation. We even added body tracking allowing users to step into and interact physically with their scene.

Diorama stills

My work centered around conceptualizing, testing and honing the UX and interactions for Diorama. I wanted to leverage the strengths of the Litho Controller for spatial interactions to create a more fluid and immersive mobile AR experience.

Diorama Touch

We created Diorama Touch as an extension of Diorama. The app does not require a Litho controller to operate and additionally allows NFT artists and collectors to show off their collection and explore others' in the real world.

Static digital artworks previously only experienced in a web browser are made dynamic and given a real-world physicality that only AR can offer.

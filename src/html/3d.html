Photogrammetry

I have been dabbling with photogrammetry (the process of constructing 3D models from photographs) for around 8 years. I have developed a workflow to scan objects in high detail. Flashes and cross-polarised light allow me to take sharp photos which in turn generate accurate scans.

NFT

I have created a couple of 3D NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. I aim for each model's textures to be devoid of any baked-in lighting data. In this sense they are 'metaverse ready' assets, able to be placed in any real or virtual environment with correct lighting.

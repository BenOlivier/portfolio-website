Customuse is a platform that enables users to create, share, and explore custom designs for the metaverse. It allows anyone to create in 3D, without the need for specialist skills and software.

Users can use the Customuse editor to design and remix avatar clothing, skins and items ready to be used in popular gaming and social platforms such as Roblox, Minecraft, TikTok, and Zepeto.

As Lead Product Designer, I completeley overhauled the design of the web + mobile editor apps, and content discovery page. I worked closely with engineers and product managers to oversee successful implementation of the new designs.

As part of the redesign, we added exciting new AI features, allowing users to leverage generative AI to create new outfits in seconds.

Customuse has seen fantastic growth, with over 3 million total creators using the platform. This success is only set to continue with the proliferation of self expression in virtual worlds and the democratisation of 3D tools.

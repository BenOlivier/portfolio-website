North Star

I built a fully functioning AR headset from 3D printed parts and available electronics. The design was open sourced by Leap Motion in 2018. It features best in class hand tracking for creating AR interactions and interfaces. A community of builders and developers has come together to share projects built for the headset and improvements to its design.
